What We Do

Full-Service Property Care, Season to Season

From the first spring cleanup to the last plow run of winter, we keep your property in shape year-round.

Seasonal Cleanups

Spring & Fall

Spring Cleanup

Leaf and debris removal, bed cleanout, lawn dethatching, and the first cut of the season — your property crisp and ready before the planting starts.

Fall Cleanup

Full property leaf removal, perennial cutbacks, and final lawn cuts. Closing your property out clean so winter doesn't bury it.

Lawn & Planting

Weekly Care

Full Lawn Service

Weekly mowing, edging, trimming, and blow-down. Reliable routes built around Guilford and the Shoreline, so your lawn stays sharp all season.

Landscape Planting

New beds, shrubs, perennials, transplants, and seasonal color — plants picked for the Shoreline's soil and shade so they actually thrive.

Mulch Work

Bed prep, edging, and fresh mulch installation in the color and depth you want. A tidy, weed-suppressing finish that lasts the season.

Tree & Pruning

By Hand & By Machine

Pruning

Shrub shaping, ornamental tree pruning, hedge work, and structural cuts — done by hand, at the right time of year for each species.

Tree Work

Removal, takedowns, deadwooding, and clearing — including tight residential lots. We bring our own equipment and clean up before we leave.

Stump Grinding

Grinding stumps below grade so you can replant, level, or just walk the lawn without tripping. Cleanup and topsoil refill included.

Brush Chipping

On-site chipping of brush, branches, and storm debris with our own chipper. Chips hauled away or left as mulch — your call.

Hardscape & Heavy Equipment

Built to Last

Tractor Work

Grading, regrading, light excavation, post-storm cleanup, and material moving — the right tractor for residential work, without tearing up your lawn.

Walkways Installed

Stone and paver walkway design and installation. Solid base prep so the work holds up through Connecticut winters — not just one season.

Year-Round Services

Winter & Beyond

Firewood

Seasoned cordwood, cut and split. Local delivery in Guilford and surrounding towns throughout the burn season.

Snow Plowing

Driveway plowing through the season, with sanding on request. Reliable callouts during storms — we plow our existing customers first.
